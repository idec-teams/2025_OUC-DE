Experimental Results

Single Mutant Screening Results

We successfully generated and screened single mutants at four key positions (R143, R159, F170, K172). The tetrasaccharide content in degradation products was measured using HPLC analysis.

Mutation Position Best Mutant Tetrasaccharide Content Improvement
R143 R143A 32.5% +7.3%
R159 R159N 46.2% +21.0%
F170 F170Y 28.9% +3.7%
K172 K172E 35.8% +10.6%

R159N showed the most significant improvement, increasing tetrasaccharide content to 46.2%, representing a 21.0% enhancement over wild-type PyAly.

Machine Learning Predictions

Using single mutant data, we trained machine learning models to predict the effects of multiple mutations. The random forest model achieved the best performance with R² = 0.89 on test data.

Four multiple-site mutants were designed based on computational predictions:

  • R159A_K172E (predicted yield: 34.91%)
  • R159N_F170Y (predicted yield: 42.18%)
  • R143A_R159N_K172E (predicted yield: 38.45%)
  • R159N_F170Y_K172E (predicted yield: 44.72%)

Multiple Mutant Validation

Experimental validation of the designed multiple mutants confirmed the predictive accuracy of our machine learning models.

Mutant Predicted Yield Experimental Yield Deviation
R159A_K172E 34.91% 35.2% +0.29%
R159N_F170Y 42.18% 41.8% -0.38%
R143A_R159N_K172E 38.45% 39.1% +0.65%
R159N_F170Y_K172E 44.72% 43.9% -0.82%

The triple mutant R159N_F170Y_K172E achieved the highest tetrasaccharide yield of 43.9%, demonstrating the effectiveness of our combined approach.
