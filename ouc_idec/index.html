Project Background & Significance

Alginate oligosaccharides (DP2-4) are like ‘golden molecules’ from the sea, with bioactivities including antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antitumor effects

But traditional methods produce only limited amounts of tetrasaccharides...

Innovative Methodology

We adopted a dual-engine strategy combining:

🔹 Structure-guided site-directed mutagenesis
🔹 Machine Learning optimal combination prediction
🔹 Experimental validation closed-loop optimization

Key Achievements

✅ Single mutant R159N: Tetrasaccharide content increased to 46.2%
✅ Double mutant R159A_K172E: Predicted yield 34.91%
✅ Machine learning models accurately predicted mutation effects
